Account & Data Deletion Request

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: From the App

  1. Open FitForge Ultimate on your device
  2. Go to More → Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your identity and tap "Delete My Account"

Option 2: Via Email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to us with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your account.

📧 Send Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed within 30 days:

  • Profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Workout history and exercise logs
  • Nutrition data and meal logs
  • Body stats and measurements
  • Progress data and personal records
  • Habits, goals, and streak data
  • Community posts and social interactions
  • Achievements, badges, and rewards
  • AI Coach conversation history
  • Fitness Wrapped data
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your fitness data, progress, streaks, and achievements will be lost forever. Please make sure you want to proceed before requesting deletion.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been fully deleted.
